Rules

4 in a row is a classic strategic board game where two players fight for glory. The looser has to pay of the winners student loans.

  • Each player gets 21 discs of a color (red or yellow).
  • Players take turn placing a disc on the board.
  • First player to get four bricks of their color in a row (vertical, horizontal or diagonally) wins.
  • The board has 42 slots. If all slots are filled and no one has gotten four in a row the game is a tie.
